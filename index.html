About Us

Aeromodelling is a hobby that combines both art and science into one form. It is a hobby of building and flying scale airplanes that can be controlled by multiple means. It is both a hobby and a sport. Sparrow Aviation was started with a vision to impart the knowledge and legacy of aeromodelling to younger generations. A membership at this club provides access to multiple airfields spread across the United States, weekly and monthly magazines, and a platform to connect with experts. Come fly with us today!
